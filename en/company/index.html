Corporate Heritage

Our Journey Since 2012

Since 2012, AppEZ has paved the way as a trusted IT infrastructure specialist. Over the past decade, we have established robust partnerships with global IT leaders like IBM, Dell Technologies, Lenovo, HPE, Nutanix, and Veeam.

Equipped with highly certified engineering talent, we have completed over 150 enterprise projects, supporting clients in building secure, high-availability data architectures. We hold official partner levels including IBM Business Partner Gold Level and Dell Gold Partner, reflecting our technical capabilities and market trust.

As a licensed telecommunications contractor, we continue to expand our cloud capabilities, hyperconverged infrastructure (HCI) services, and smart IT observability, adapting to the modern hybrid cloud landscape.
