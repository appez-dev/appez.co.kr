Privacy Policy

Learn how AppEZ Co., Ltd. protects and manages your personal information.

1. Purpose of Collection and Use

AppEZ Co., Ltd. ("Company") collects and uses minimal personal information solely for providing business services, including inquiry consultation, system integration, and operations maintenance.

2. Items of Personal Information Collected

The Company collects the following information during inquiries:

  • Required Items: Company/Organization Name, Contact Person Name, Email Address, Message Content.

3. Retention and Use Period

In principle, the collected personal information is destroyed immediately after the consultation is completed. However, if retention is required by laws, the information is stored for the period specified by the relevant legislation.

4. Destruction Procedure and Method

The Company safely destroys personal information after the retention period:

  • Electronic files: Permanently deleted using technical methods that make records irrecoverable.
  • Paper printouts: Shredded using a paper shredder.

5. Rights of Information Subjects

Information subjects may request to view, correct, delete, or suspend processing of their personal information at any time by contacting us via email (info@appez.co.kr).
